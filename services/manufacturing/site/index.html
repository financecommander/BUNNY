FAQ

What buyers usually want to know before handing over a part request.

Are you a factory?

No. Buildmesh is a private manufacturing collective and execution partner. We own intake, review, placement judgment, and delivery follow-through across a distributed supply base.

What kinds of jobs fit best?

Fixtures, brackets, housings, replacement parts, functional plastic parts, and low-volume builds where sourcing friction is higher than normal.

What should we send?

A part file if you have it, plus quantity, timing, finish needs, and any constraints around fit, material, or use environment.

How quickly do you respond?

For qualified requests, Buildmesh aims to respond within one business day with the next best step, whether that is review, quote development, or a clarification request.
