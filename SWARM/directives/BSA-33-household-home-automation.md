# BSA-33 — Household AI & Home Automation Directive

**System:** Calculus AI Platform
**Authority:** BUNNY Core (Home Intelligence Operations) + BUNNY Shield (Privacy & Safety Enforcement)
**Component:** Jenny Household Intelligence Layer
**Directive Type:** Smart Home Integration, Domestic Automation, and Household Coordination

---

## 1. Purpose

This directive establishes the Household AI & Home Automation capabilities for the Calculus AI platform through the Jenny personal assistant agent.

Jenny must be capable of coordinating and assisting with home systems, family activities, and domestic automation.

The system must enable Jenny to function as:
- A home automation coordinator
- A household scheduling assistant
- A home monitoring system
- A family productivity assistant

Jenny's household functions must remain separate from enterprise swarm systems.

## 2. Household Intelligence Model

```
Household Devices
    ↓
Home Automation Gateway
    ↓
Jenny Household Agent
    ↓
Personal Swarm Node
    ↓
User Interface
```

Jenny acts as the central intelligence layer for home systems.

## 3. Supported Smart Home Systems

### Lighting Systems
- Philips Hue, LIFX, Smart switches

**Capabilities:** Light scheduling, room-based lighting, scenes, automatic adjustments

### Climate Control
- Nest, Ecobee, Smart thermostats

**Capabilities:** Temperature scheduling, energy optimization, remote control

### Security Systems
- Ring, Arlo, Nest cameras, smart door locks, alarm systems

**Capabilities:** Security alerts, camera monitoring, door lock control, visitor notifications

### Home Appliances
- Smart refrigerators, washing machines, ovens, robot vacuums

**Capabilities:** Status monitoring, scheduled operation, maintenance alerts
