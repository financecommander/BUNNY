# BSA-34 — Personal Finance & Life Management Directive

**System:** Calculus AI Platform
**Authority:** BUNNY Core (Personal Intelligence) + BUNNY Shield (Financial Security & Privacy)
**Component:** Jenny Personal Finance & Life Management Layer
**Directive Type:** Financial Organization, Life Logistics, and Personal Resource Management

---

## 1. Purpose

This directive establishes the Personal Finance & Life Management capabilities for the Calculus AI platform through the Jenny personal assistant agent.

Jenny must help individuals and families organize financial information, monitor expenses, manage bills, and coordinate life logistics.

Jenny becomes a financial awareness and life-management assistant, helping users maintain visibility into:
- Spending and budgeting
- Bill schedules
- Subscriptions
- Financial commitments
- Long-term goals

Jenny must never operate as a financial advisor or trading system unless explicitly integrated with approved financial intelligence modules.

## 2. Personal Finance Intelligence Model

```
Financial Data Capture
    ↓
Classification
    ↓
Budget Analysis
    ↓
Expense Monitoring
    ↓
Bill & Subscription Tracking
    ↓
Financial Insight Generation
    ↓
User Notification
```

Each event generates a Financial Intelligence Record.

## 3. Financial Data Sources

Jenny may collect financial information from user-authorized systems.

**Examples:**
- Bank account summaries
- Credit card statements
- Payment apps
- Personal spreadsheets
- Manual user input

**Supported Integrations:**
- Bank transaction exports
- CSV or Excel budget sheets
- Personal finance apps
- Secure API banking connectors (if authorized)

All integrations must require explicit user permission.

## 4. Budget Tracking

**Budget Categories:**
- Housing
- Food
- Transportation
- Utilities
- Subscriptions
- Entertainment
- Savings

**Example:** "Jenny, show my spending for groceries this month."

Jenny provides summaries and visual breakdowns.

## 5. Expense Monitoring

**Monitoring Functions:**
- Spending categorization
- Monthly expense totals
- Unusual spending alerts
- Trend analysis

**Example Alert:** "Your dining expenses are 35% higher than last month."

Jenny must monitor progress and provide updates.

## 13. Financial Privacy & Security

**Security Policies:**
- Encrypted storage
- Secure authentication
- Device-level security
- Restricted data access

Jenny must never expose financial data to external systems without user consent. BUNNY Shield enforces all financial security policies.

## 14. Financial Insight Generation

**Examples:**
- Spending trends
- Budget deviations
- Savings progress
- Subscription waste

**Example:** "You could save $200 per month by canceling two unused subscriptions."

Insights must remain advisory rather than prescriptive.
