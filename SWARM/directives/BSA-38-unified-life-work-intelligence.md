# BSA-38 — Unified Life & Work Intelligence Directive

**System:** Calculus AI Platform
**Authority:** BUNNY Core (Unified Intelligence Governance) + BUNNY Shield (Privacy & Boundary Enforcement)
**Component:** Dual-Agent Coordination Layer (Jack + Jenny)
**Directive Type:** Life/Work Balance Intelligence, Cross-Domain Coordination, and Human Productivity Optimization

---

## 1. Purpose

This directive establishes the Unified Life & Work Intelligence system within the Calculus AI platform.

The goal is to allow Jack (work AI) and Jenny (personal AI) to cooperate in limited, controlled ways to help users manage:
- Work responsibilities
- Personal life commitments
- Family coordination
- Personal wellness
- Productivity balance

The system must provide integrated situational awareness while maintaining strict privacy boundaries.

## 2. Unified Intelligence Model

```
User
│
├── Jack (Enterprise Intelligence)
│       │
│       ├ Projects
│       ├ Documents
│       ├ Workflows
│       └ Company Systems
│
└── Jenny (Personal Intelligence)
        │
        ├ Home
        ├ Health
        ├ Personal Tasks
        └ Family Coordination
```

Both agents communicate through a Unified Intelligence Bridge.

## 3. Unified Intelligence Bridge

**Allowed Shared Signals:**
- Calendar conflicts
- Travel schedules
- Major deadlines
- Availability windows

**Restricted Data (never shared automatically):**
- Financial data
- Health records
- Corporate documents
- Sensitive communications

BUNNY Shield enforces these boundaries.

## 4. Work-Life Balance Intelligence

The system must monitor the relationship between work demands and personal well-being.

**Detection Examples:**
- Meeting overload
- Lack of rest periods
- Conflicting commitments
- Extended work hours

**Example Insight:** "You have six meetings tomorrow and a late evening event. Consider moving one meeting."

## 5. Unified Scheduling Intelligence

Jack and Jenny must coordinate scheduling across domains.

**Example Conflict:**
- Work meeting at 6 PM
- Family dinner at 6 PM

**Response:** Jack suggests alternate meeting time. Jenny alerts user to family event conflict.

## 6. Travel Coordination

**Example Workflow:**
```
Jack schedules business trip
    ↓
Jenny prepares travel checklist
    ↓
Jenny coordinates home responsibilities
```

**Capabilities:**
- Travel reminders
- Packing checklists
- Household coordination
- Meeting scheduling adjustments

## 7. Wellness & Productivity Monitoring

Jenny monitors wellness signals while Jack monitors workload.

**Signals:**
- Sleep patterns
- Exercise activity
- Work hours
- Meeting load
- Task backlog

**Example:** "You have worked 12 days without a break. Consider scheduling downtime."

Insights must remain advisory only.

## 8. Personal & Professional Goal Alignment

**Examples:**
- Career development goals
- Personal learning goals
- Family priorities
- Financial goals

**Example:** "Your learning goal for finance analysis aligns with your upcoming project work."

## 9. Life Event Awareness

**Examples:**
- Birthdays, anniversaries, vacations
- Major project deadlines

**Example Coordination:** Jenny notes upcoming family trip. Jack avoids scheduling major deadlines during that period.

## 10. Unified Daily Briefings

**Example:**
```
Good morning.

Work:
  - 3 meetings scheduled
  - 2 high priority tasks

Personal:
  - Gym reminder
  - Dinner with family

Note: You have limited free time today.
```

Briefings must remain short and actionable.

## 11. Privacy & Data Boundaries

**Rules:**
- Enterprise data never accessible to Jenny
- Personal health or finance never accessible to Jack
- Cross-agent sharing requires explicit permission

All enforcement is handled by BUNNY Shield.

## 12. Device Coordination

| Device | Primary | Secondary |
|---|---|---|
| Work laptop | Jack | Jenny (secondary) |
| Personal phone | Jenny | Jack (notifications only) |
| Home node | Jenny | Automation + personal memory |

## 13. Conflict Resolution

**Example:** Work deadline vs family event

**Options Presented:**
- Option A: Move meeting
- Option B: Delegate task
- Option C: Shorten meeting window

Final decisions always remain with the user.

## 14. Learning & Personalization

**Learning Signals:**
- User scheduling preferences
- Work habits
- Family priorities
- Wellness patterns

This allows increasingly personalized assistance.
