# BSA-35 — Personal Health & Wellness Intelligence Directive

**System:** Calculus AI Platform
**Authority:** BUNNY Core (Personal Wellness Intelligence) + BUNNY Shield (Health Data Privacy & Safety)
**Component:** Jenny Health & Wellness Intelligence Layer
**Directive Type:** Health Tracking, Wellness Assistance, and Personal Care Coordination

---

## 1. Purpose

This directive establishes the Personal Health & Wellness Intelligence system for the Calculus AI platform through the Jenny personal assistant agent.

Jenny must assist users in maintaining awareness and organization of personal health and wellness information.

Capabilities include helping users manage:
- Medical appointments
- Medication reminders
- Fitness activities
- Sleep and wellness tracking
- Health goals

Jenny functions as a wellness support assistant, helping users stay organized and informed about their personal health routines.

Jenny must not replace professional medical advice.

## 2. Health Intelligence Framework

```
Health Data Capture
    ↓
Health Activity Tracking
    ↓
Goal Monitoring
    ↓
Reminder Generation
    ↓
Insight Generation
    ↓
User Notification
```

Each entry generates a Health Intelligence Record.

## 3. Health Data Sources

Jenny may collect health-related information from user-authorized sources.

**Examples:**
- Manual user input
- Fitness tracker integrations
- Mobile health apps
- Calendar-based appointments

**Device Integrations:**
- Apple Health
- Google Fit
- Fitbit
- Garmin devices

All health data integrations must require explicit user permission.

## 4. Medical Appointment Management

**Examples:**
- Doctor visits
- Dental appointments
- Therapy sessions
- Specialist consultations

**Capabilities:**
- Calendar scheduling
- Appointment reminders
- Location and preparation notes

**Example:** "You have a dental appointment tomorrow at 10:30 AM."

## 5. Medication Reminders

**Capabilities:**
- Medication reminders
- Dosage schedules
- Refill alerts

**Example:** "Reminder: Take your medication at 8 PM."

Jenny must not provide medical dosage advice beyond user-supplied instructions.

## 6. Fitness Activity Tracking

**Examples:**
- Daily step counts
- Workout sessions
- Running or cycling logs

**Example:** "Jenny, log my workout today."

Jenny stores and summarizes activity data.

## 7. Wellness Goal Tracking

**Examples:**
- Daily step goals
- Exercise frequency
- Sleep targets
- Hydration goals

**Example Goal:** 8,000 steps per day

Jenny monitors progress and provides encouragement.

## 8. Sleep Tracking

**Capabilities:**
- Sleep duration tracking
- Sleep schedule reminders
- Sleep quality summaries

**Example Insight:** "You slept an average of 6.2 hours this week."

## 9. Wellness Insights

**Examples:**
- Exercise trends
- Sleep consistency
- Activity patterns

**Example:** "You tend to exercise more consistently on weekdays."

Insights must remain informational rather than medical recommendations.

## 10. Health Document Organization

**Document Types:**
- Medical test results
- Insurance documents
- Prescriptions
- Vaccination records

**Storage:**
- Secure personal storage
- Health record folders
- Encrypted archives

## 11. Health Briefings

**Example:**
```
Weekly Wellness Summary:
  - Average sleep: 7.1 hours
  - Workouts completed: 3
  - Step goal achieved: 4 days
```

These briefings help users monitor wellness progress.

## 12. Emergency Contact Assistance

**Examples:**
- Family contacts
- Primary doctor
- Emergency services

**Example:** "Jenny, store my doctor's contact information."

## 13. Privacy & Health Data Protection

**Security Policies:**
- Encrypted health data storage
- Restricted user access
- Device-level authentication

Jenny must never share health information externally without explicit permission. BUNNY Shield enforces health data protection.

## 14. Wellness Reminders

**Examples:**
- Hydration reminders
- Stretching breaks
- Sleep schedule reminders
- Exercise prompts

**Example:** "You've been working for two hours. Consider taking a short break."
