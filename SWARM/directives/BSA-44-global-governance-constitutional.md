# BSA-44 — Global Swarm Governance & Constitutional Layer

**System:** Calculus AI Platform
**Authority:** BUNNY Core (System Governance) + BUNNY Shield (Security, Compliance & Enforcement)
**Component:** Global Governance & Constitutional Framework
**Directive Type:** System Rules, Ethical Boundaries, Policy Enforcement, and Dispute Resolution

---

## 1. Purpose

This directive establishes the constitutional governance framework for the Calculus AI ecosystem.

The goal is to define foundational rules, principles, and policies governing:
- Swarm operations
- Agent behavior
- Node participation
- Economic systems
- Cross-swarm collaboration

The constitutional layer ensures the swarm operates safely, transparently, and consistently at scale.
